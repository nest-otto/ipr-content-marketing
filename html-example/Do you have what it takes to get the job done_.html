Equipment and Efficiency

Do you have what it takes to get the job done?

The wrong equipment can make your job more difficult and poor scheduling or irregular pickups can compound the problem.

This raises the question: do you have what you need for things to run smoothly?

When it comes to waste storage, some equipment choices can end up making more work.

Open-air waste containers placed outdoors are open to the elements, at the mercy of rain, dust and even wildlife, allowing paper and cardboard to go flying on a windy day. Cleaning up this mess is a poor use of valuable resources. For many businesses, long delays between pickups can also add to the burden of waste storage.

Every business has specific equipment and scheduling needs.

By providing a broader range of services than other suppliers, International Paper can help clients recycle more types of waste. With the placement of proper equipment tailored to your specific situation, waste can be sorted, weighed, and graded by type, eliminating mess and improving the efficiency and sustainability of your business.
